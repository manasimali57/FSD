Pune City Transport


Information

Pune offers a variety of transport options including PMPML buses, local trains, and metro. Auto-rickshaws, taxis, and ride-sharing services like Uber and Ola are widely available. The Pune International Airport handles domestic and some international flights. Cycling and walking are encouraged with rental services and pedestrian pathways. Regular intercity buses connect Pune to major nearby cities.
