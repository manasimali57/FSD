Engineering college in Pune

MIT World Peace University

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU), located in Pune, India, is a prominent educational institution established under the umbrella of the Maharashtra Institute of Technology (MIT) Group, founded in 1983 by Dr. Vishwanath Karad. MIT-WPU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including engineering, management, science, arts, commerce, law, and design. It is renowned for integrating academic rigor with a focus on holistic education, emphasizing values, ethics, and global peace

Savitribai Phule Pune University

Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU), established in 1949, is one of India's leading educational institutions, located in Pune, Maharashtra. Named after Savitribai Phule, a pioneer in women's education, SPPU offers a wide array of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines such as science, arts, commerce, law, and engineering. The university is noted for its robust academic programs and is affiliated with numerous colleges

Symbiosis International University

Symbiosis International University (SIU), located in Pune, Maharashtra, is a premier educational institution established in 2002. It is part of the Symbiosis Society, founded by Dr. S. B. Mujumdar in 1971. SIU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including management, law, health sciences, engineering, media, and arts

The College of Engineering, Pune

The College of Engineering, Pune (COEP), established in 1854, is one of India's oldest and most prestigious engineering institutions. Located in Pune, Maharashtra, COEP has a rich history of academic excellence and innovation in engineering education. The college offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various engineering disciplines, including civil, mechanical, electrical, computer, and information technology

Bharati Vidyapeeth

Bharati Vidyapeeth, established in 1964 by Dr. Patangrao Kadam, is a prominent educational institution in Pune, India. It offers a diverse array of programs in fields such as engineering, medicine, law, management, arts, and science. The institution operates multiple campuses, with the main one in Pune and others across India, including Navi Mumbai and New Delhi. Recognized as a deemed university by the University Grants Commission (UGC), Bharati Vidyapeeth has the authority to grant its own degrees and design curricula tailored to industry and academic standards.

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT), established in 1983 in Pune, Maharashtra, is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University. Medical college in Pune

Armed Forces Medical College Pune

Armed Forces Medical College is a premier medical institute of India acknowledged as a centre of excellence for education and research. The college provides training to under-graduate and post-graduate medical and nursing students with assured career prospects in the defence services.

Dr. D. Y. Patil Medical College

Dr. D. Y. Patil Medical College in Pune is a premier institution offering MBBS, MD, and MS programs. It's part of Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth and is known for its advanced medical facilities and research focus.

byramjee jeejeebhoy medical college

Byramjee Jeejeebhoy Medical College (BJMC) in Pune is a leading medical institution in India. It offers MBBS and various postgraduate programs. BJMC is affiliated with Maharashtra University of Health Sciences and is known for its excellent medical education, research, and healthcare services.

symbiosis women's medical college

Symbiosis Medical College for Women (SMCW) in Pune is a prestigious institution dedicated to medical education for women. It offers the MBBS program and is part of Symbiosis International (Deemed University). The college focuses on providing high-quality medical education, research opportunities, and healthcare services.

kashibai navale college of engineering

Kashibai Navale Medical College (KNMC) in Pune is a well-known institution offering MBBS and various postgraduate medical programs. It is affiliated with Maharashtra University of Health Sciences and is recognized for its advanced medical facilities, quality education, and research initiatives.

medical college bharati vidyapeeth pune
